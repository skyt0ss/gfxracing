# GFX★RACING

Ottawa's indoor remote-control car racing facility. We run three scale-class tracks — **1/28**, **1/10**, and **1/12** — under one roof at **2481 Kaladar Ave, Unit 103**, giving drivers of every skill level a dedicated place to compete, practice, and improve year-round.

## Our Facility

The GFX★RACING facility features purpose-built indoor carpet tracks engineered for precision R/C racing. Climate-controlled year-round, our smooth technical layouts reward car setup and driver skill equally. Whether you're pushing for the fastest lap or learning the basics, you'll find a track that matches your pace.

## Our Community

GFX★RACING started with a small group of Ottawa hobbyists who wanted a serious venue for competitive R/C. Today we have a diverse membership spanning beginners, intermediates, and seasoned competitors — all united by a shared obsession with small-scale speed.

We welcome everyone:

- **First-time racers** — staff and veteran members help with setup and rule questions
- **Regular competitors** — weekly race days with structured class formats
- **Casual practice drivers** — open practice sessions most days
- **All ages** — from juniors to the 50+ class on track every weekend
